As you scope and fine tune your app, you should also be preparing for your app certification and scheduling a demo with the Concur partner enablement team to showcase your app. Below are key resource documents to help you through this process.

As you prepare to launch your app you’ll have various marketing assets and activities to prepare. Below are key resource documents to guide you through your marketing planning.


App Center Partner Listing Form: Review to prepare your app for listing in the Concur App Center. Your listing will serve as the location where companies and users will learn more about your app, leading to inquiries or end user connections.

Concur Brand Guidelines: Become familiar with our Concur brand guidelines prior to developing your various marketing assets, which can include a partner landing page, brochures or videos.
