Setting up a sandbox

Sandboxes are required for partners or customers looking to use Concur's APIs to read and write data from Concur. It is important that you contact your assigned technical resource at Concur to gain access to a sandbox that best fits your scenario.

If you are unsure of your technical contact, you can reach out on the Concur Developer Forums and someone will be able to help identify the right resource for you. Indicate in your forum Post one of these 3 goals:

  • 1. develop an application for the Concur App Center. This requires a signed Partner Agreement.
  • 2. develop an application as a Concur client (or for a client). The client must purchase the Web Services SKU.
  • 3. devleop an application as a TMC/reseller.
